ABOUT

We are team who started real small and who grew up around families who love to bake. As time passed we got fully equipped with family recipes that got passed down from previous generations and finally decided to come together and put it to good use. We started a baking business using tools from home and started producing cake aswell as other baked goods. However we struggled to get customers and businesses to buy our goods.

We decided to then take our business on the road , not knowing that this would be the turning point of our bakery business. This is what we did to please the customers that we had and it got our business up and running better than ever.

Today we own our own modern bakery. We also host everything from social and business events to small intimate weddings and functions. When we initially started our business , we never thought we would become this big.
